What We Do

Michigan GIVErs incorporates four main components into our club values: fundraising, philanthropy, outreach, and social. By planning events and programs focused in these areas, we have built up a strong portfolio of things to get invovled with in our club!


Discover more about what we do in Michigan GIVErs below.

Fundraising


Fundraising is one of the main components of our club. We organize fundraisers so that club members can earn money to put towards the cost of their trips.


Some of our past fundraisers include selling Krispy Kreme donuts during tailgating before a Michigan Football game, selling Chick-Fil-A in the Dude on North Campus, doing a Halloween and St. Patty's Day Balloon-Pop fundraiser outside Charley's where people can pop balloons and win a prize, and selling food during Hash Bash!


Philanthropy


Giving back to the community around our campus is also an important part of our club. We participate in philanthropy events surrounding the topics of sustainability, education, and wildlife conservation.


Past philanthropy events include volunteering at the Creature Conservancy, participating in a recycling event at the Ann Arbor ReUse Center, educating an elementary class about being global citizens, and volunteering at a Food Gatherers.


Outreach


Beyond philanthropy events, we also like to put on Outreach events and programs in order to bring awareness to relevant topics within our campus community.

We've done movie screenings about Coral Reef Conservation, tabling for Plastic Awareness Week, and learned about refugees with MRAP.


Social


We love having social events so that we can get to know our fellow Michigan GIVErs! We're more than just club members, we've created lifelong friendships through our shared interests.


Each year we hold a GIVE alumni dinner, we've held a game night, cooked authentic food from a global country, and even went on a trip to the Smokey Mountains!

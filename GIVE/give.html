G.I.V.E.

Michigan GIVErs is a club based off a company called GIVE (Growth International Voluteer Excursions) that sends college students to 5 different developing countries to volunteer and adventure.


While on a trip, you can expect to volunteer on projects related to sustainability, English education, and wildlife conservation, as well as exploring some of the coolest things each country has to offer while feeling immersed in the culture of life abroad as a global citizen.


GIVE countries include: Nicaragua, Tanzania, Thailand, Laos, and Nepal!

Nicaragua


Visit: Little Corn Island & Jiquillio


Volunteer initiatives: English Education, sustainable housing and school buildings, Invasive Species Prevention, Hawksbill Turtle conservation


Adventure Activities: Sandboard down an active volcano, learn to surf, speak Spanish with locals


Add On's: Scuba Diving Reef Conservation Excursion

Tanzania


Visit: Zanzibar Island & Arusha


Volunteer initiatives: English Education, eco-friendly wells and sustainable school buildings


Adventure Activities: Swim with wild dolphins, go on a 2-day Safari, Sailing/Snorkeling day, visit a Masaii Village


Add On's: Climb Mt. Kilimanjaro

Thailand


Visit: Muang Khong & Chiang Mai


Volunteer initiatives: English Education, Sustainable bridge and drinking/hygiene station, Elephant & Forest Conservation


Adventure Activities: Build a bamboo raft and go on a 2-day float trip, play with elephants, work in rice paddies, visit ancient temple ruins, 50M bungee jump


Add On's: Visit Laos

Laos


Visit: Luang Prabang & Sop Chem


Volunteer initiatives: English Education, eco-friendly Dorms and water tank, Moon Bear Conservation


Adventure Activities: Learn Meditation from local monks, participate in an Alms ceremony, morning yoga, hike to a moutain village to spend a night with a local family, visit waterfalls, & MORE


Add On's: Visit Thailand

Nepal


Visit: Remote Villages & Bhaktapur


Volunteer initiatives: English Education, Reconstruction of homes after 2015 earthquake


Adventure Activities: 2-day white water rafting trip, wildlife safari (part by canoe, part by foot), explore ancient Hindu Kingdom, watch sunrise over Himalyan Moutains, learn to cook from locals


Add On's: Climb to Mt. Everest Base Camp

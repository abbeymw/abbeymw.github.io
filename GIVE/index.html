The purpose of the Michigan GIVErs is to encourage UofM students to participate in the improvement of underprivileged communities through sustainability initiatives and to educate students on the initiatives of the GIVE organization. The Michigan GIVErs will encourage involvement in local community service as well as help create opportunities for students to volunteer abroad through G.I.V.E. and help the international GIVE communities through work on campus. This will be achieved through fundraisers, donation collections, and organized activities.


We welcome any UofM student to join our club! Take a look around to discover more about Michigan GIVErs. Contact us if you are interested or have any questions.
